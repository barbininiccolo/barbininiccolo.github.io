# Why this website?

As a **Software Engineer**, I like to build new things. In particular, I wanted to put into practice what I have learnt over the past few years. After starting to move from an academic way of coding to a more professional approach, I decided to experiment with Svelte, TypeScript, and Tailwind CSS to build something of my own.
Currently, this website has sections that let people know who I am. However, I would like to start posting some content in the blog section.
